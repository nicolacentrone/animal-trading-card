Arctic Wolf

The Arctic Wolf's white hair permits to camouflage himself in the environment covered with snow.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus arctos
  • Average lenght: 1,6 meters
  • Weight: Up to 45 kg
  • Average lifespan: 7-10 years

The Arctic wolf is a subspecies of gray-wolf which is native to the Canadian Arctic Archipelago. It's carnivorous, lives in packs and it has a strong body made to survive in hard conditions. Be careful if you meet one of these animals, they're not as cute as they might look.
